Solar Production vs. Air Quality

The hypothesis for this research is: If production of solar energy is related to air quality then increased solar production will lower CO2 emissions.

The conclusion based on a very low r-squared value of 0.10 is to reject the hypothesis. There was little to no correclation between solar production and lower CO2 emissions.

When looking at 5 states of interest it appears there is a correlation showing states with higher solar production actually have higher emissions. This is the exact opposite of the original hypothesis and we conclude this to be incorrect. The sample is too small to make this a true conclusion and the outlier of Texas really skews the data
